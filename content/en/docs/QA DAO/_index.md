Quality Assurance DAO (QADAO) began in April 2021 with an Open Source Feedback survey and submitted a proposal "[Quality-Assurance-DAO](https://cardano.ideascale.com/c/idea/351678)" in the [Fund 5: Developer ecosystem Challenge](https://cardano.ideascale.com/c/campaigns/25939/about). After this proposal was successfully funded QADAO documented the pathway of a funded cohort & began to assess Project Catalyst’s reporting requirements.
