## Catalyst Circle Meeting 8 was held on Thursday, 21st October 2021

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/vzuYlhG5zxQ" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>

A recording with timestamps and minutes of Catalyst Circle Meeting 8 held on Thursday, 21st October 2021 is now available on the [QA-DAO Circle Oversight GitBook](https://catalyst-swarm.gitbook.io/catalyst-circle/meetings/meeting-8-october-21st-2021) and as a [Google Document here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1C8GGAmo9fUC6cX-o35XZwBIrN0zZsZahY8HjzR2jcLY/edit?usp=sharing).

This meeting was the last Circle Meeting of Version 1. Elections for Circle Version 2 will take place on November 6th, 2021

Catalyst Circle Meeting 8 discussed the following :

- IOG: How does IOHK and Catalyst community coordinate building the developer and governance eco-system effectively?
- IOG: How do we remove IOHK from every decision made in Catalyst? How can we transition from governors to service providers?
- Auditability
- A Retrospective of Circle Meeting Version 1 : Things we’ve Loved, Learned, Lacked, Longed for and List of things to note
